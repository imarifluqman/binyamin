Manufacturing

Binyamin Steel currently has fully operational steel manufacturing units in Pakistan. This includes a state-of- the art integrated steel plant at Quetta, with facilities like DRI Unit, EAF, Continuous Billet Casting Mill, sophisticated and high-speed Rolling Mill, sophisticated and high-speed Rolling Mill, microprocessor based product technologies and fully-equipped Quality Assurance & Testing laboratories. together as a single cohesive unit.
